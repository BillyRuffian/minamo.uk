Residential / 2024

Urban Sanctuary

A serene downtown apartment featuring clean lines and natural materials. This project demonstrates our commitment to creating peaceful urban retreats that balance modern aesthetics with functional living spaces.

Project Details

Location

London, UK

Year

2024

Area

120 m²

Type

Residential

Design Approach

Our approach for this urban apartment centered on creating a sense of tranquility within a bustling city environment. We carefully selected materials that would bring warmth and texture while maintaining the clean, minimal aesthetic our client desired.

The open-plan living area flows seamlessly into private spaces, with subtle spatial divisions created through thoughtful furniture placement and changes in material palette. Natural light was maximized through strategic window treatments and reflective surfaces.

Materials & Finishes

  • White oak flooring throughout
  • Venetian plaster walls
  • Natural linen curtains
  • Limestone countertops
  • Matte black fixtures
  • Custom millwork in walnut
